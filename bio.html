Biography

The story behind the music

Quick Facts

  • Pianist, Composer, Arranger
  • American
  • New Age & Classical
  • Multiple Albums & Singles

Daniel Ketchum is a talented American pianist, composer, and arranger known for blending classical influences (such as Mozart) with new age and inspirational styles.

His music creates immersive piano landscapes that evoke a sense of mystery, wonder, and emotional depth, offering listeners tranquility and introspection.

Early Life & Training

Growing up in a home filled with classical music and jazz, Ketchum discovered his passion for piano at age 12. He trained under pianist Lisa Tharp and drew inspiration from artists like David Lanz. By his teens, he was already composing and performing original works.

Musical Style

His compositions mix intricate classical structures with uplifting, spiritual new age elements, exploring themes of tranquility, healing, and natureâ€”like misty journeys or echoing resonances.

Notable Releases

Notable releases include Sea of Tranquility (2019), a two-track album with lush piano and strings praised for awe-inspiring melodies; Resonance of Echoes (2016), recognized for thought-provoking, mood-shifting qualities that heal the mind; and singles like "Among the Mist," "Beyond the Void," "Life is Love," "Hounds of the Barrier," and "Sands of Time."

Where to Listen

His music is available on major platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, Pandora, SoundCloud, and Bandcamp. He engages fans via his website ketchummusic.com and Facebook (@poemsofnotes).

Daniel takes pride in crafting timeless, meaningful experiences that resonate deeply with listeners seeking peace and emotional connection.
