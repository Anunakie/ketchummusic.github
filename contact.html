Frequently Asked Questions

Can I license your music for my project?

Yes! I'm open to licensing discussions for films, videos, podcasts, and other creative projects. Please reach out with details about your project.

Do you offer private piano lessons?

Currently, I focus on composition and recording, but feel free to inquire about potential teaching opportunities.

How can I stay updated on new releases?

Follow me on social media or subscribe to the newsletter on the homepage to receive updates about new music and sheet music releases.

Are you available for live performances?

I occasionally perform at select venues and events. Contact me with details about your event for availability.
